Responsive web redesign

Client: U.S. Department of Education (student project)

Summary

In this student project, my team researched, prototyped and tested a responsive web redesign for a U.S. government agency homepage.

The problem

The U.S. Department of Education homepage:

  1. Lacked a clear target audience
  2. Had complex organization and navigation systems
  3. Was visually dense and unappealing

Result: an intimidating and confusing user experience.

Research

The team's first and most difficult challenge: Who is using this website and why?

Hypothesis: People looking for federal financial aid make up the majority of users.
